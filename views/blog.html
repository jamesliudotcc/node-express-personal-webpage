I used to have a blog.

I used to have a blog and I will have a blog again. It was on WordPress, when it was an open-source competitor to Typescript. Maybe you heard of it. I hosted it on Textdrive, which was eventually acquired by Joyent, which was an early cloud infrastructure company. I purchased lifetime hosting from Textdrive when it was using user-funding as its startup capital. After Joyent acquired Textdrive, things were fine for a while until they decided it was the end of life of the Textdrive product.

Joyent. Ugh.
